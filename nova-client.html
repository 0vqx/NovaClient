What Happened

A SETBACK WE DIDN'T SEE COMING

We need to be fully honest with our community. Earlier this year, a former developer with access to our infrastructure betrayed the entire team.

This person used their internal access to rat the launcher — leaking builds and project files externally. They then coordinated a raid on our Discord server, wiping channels, removing members, and destroying months of community progress in a single night.

We lost nearly everything overnight. But the core team regrouped, locked every access point, and started over from scratch. If you're here, you're part of the rebuild. Join the Discord and help us come back stronger than before.
